About Me

Information

I always wanted to have a peaceful life, where my existence would not bother anybody or harm them. Despite not having any ambition or any scope in life, I am still trying to have my best youth life as possible.

Ahhh! Almost forgot to introduce, my name is Dorji Wangchuk, I was born in a village named Thragom, Tashi Yangtse Dzongkhag.

Even though I come from a small village tucked away in the mountains, I’ve always believed that simplicity is not a limitation. In fact, it is a kind of quiet freedom. My days may not be filled with grand adventures or ambitions, but they are filled with small moments—moments that I have learned to appreciate deeply.

For now, I am just trying to live my youth with a quiet joy. I may not be achieving much by the world’s standards, but I laugh with my friends, share stories by the fire, walk under the stars, and help my family when I can. And maybe, just maybe, that is enough.

It was here that I began my educational journey and learned the fundamental etiquettes and values that shaped my character. The supportive teachers and friendly classmates made learning enjoyable and motivated me to work hard. This school laid the foundation for my academic growth and instilled in me a lifelong love for knowledge

Throughout my time at Thragom Lower Secondary, I developed a strong sense of discipline and curiosity. The lessons I learned extended beyond textbooks — I learned about teamwork, respect, and the importance of perseverance. I continued my studies at Baylling Central School, where I completed my education up to class 12. This school played a significant role in shaping my academic mindset and personal growth. The competitive environment and diverse student body challenged me to push my limits and develop resilience.

This experience strengthened my belief that education is the key to unlocking future opportunities, and it inspired me to set higher goals for myself.

Samtse College of Education

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Education (Secondary) in Information Technology at Samtse College of Education, which is part of the Royal University of Bhutan. This program is carefully crafted to prepare future teachers to effectively instruct students in coding and digital literacy from grades VII to X.

During my studies at Samtse College of Education, I am gaining skills in programming languages, database management, and various ICT applications. The curriculum combines educational theory with hands-on practice, focusing on cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence and mostly coding.

This course of study goes beyond just learning technical skills; it’s about preparing myself to inspire and equip students with the knowledge and abilities they need to succeed in an increasingly digital world.

Projects

Adobe Photoshop

For my past project, I worked extensively with Adobe Photoshop to create a visually engaging and professionally polished graphic design piece. The main objective of the project was to enhance my skills in photo editing and digital design while producing a creative output that demonstrates my understanding of Photoshop tools and techniques.

I began the project by selecting a high-resolution image, which I edited using various Photoshop features such as layer masks, adjustment layers, the pen tool, and blending modes. I applied color correction techniques to balance tones and enhance image quality, and I used retouching tools like the healing brush and clone stamp to remove imperfections.

This project helped me build a stronger understanding of Photoshop’s interface and its wide range of design capabilities.
